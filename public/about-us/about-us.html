Our Vision

BNPS cyber cell will serve as a platform for cyber-victims to receive prompt solutions to their problems, allowing them to be good cybersafe citizens. The cyber cell at our school will be extremely helpful in developing better cybersafe citizens among our students. We will also educate them on cyber security and assist them in responding to any major cyber threat as soon as possible. We will work with students to help them develop the necessary safety skills for navigating the digital world.

Our Aim

  • To spread awareness among students about various cyber crimes, ways to protect themselves, and how to avoid them.
  • To give emotional support to the students suffering from different types of cyber crime.
  • To help students suffering from different types of cyber crime.
  • Spread awareness through assemblies, different kinds of mass events, such as educating students about cyber security in the classroom.
